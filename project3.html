Chosen Weak Signals in relation to “My Fight”

Inequality, capitalism and inaccess to fair housing resources have resulted in policies and societal behaviour that favour some and discriminate against others fostering poverty and cultural inequality . In the context of Barcelona the socio-economic issue of access to resources These socio-economic ills have materialised into a housing exclusion crisis where an intervention is needed that sparkes hope and innovation for radical integration back into society of those at risk.

Methodology

On 24th of October 2021, I joined a volunteering initiative from Meet up.com called Esparanca Barcelona, that walked around El Raval and its surroundings giving out food and clothes to the homeless. This volunteering was pursued as a research driver to understand factors such as:

The extent of extreme housing exclusion in the form of homelessness in a 1km radius where I lived at the time The walking route as clues why the homeless sleep where they do, how these spaces perform better for them Draw conclusions about the needs of those on the streets and compare the found data to my assumptions before the initiative

Ideas for preliminary model / intervention

La Xarxa d'Atenció a Persones Sense Llar (XAPSLL) or The Homeless Care Network categorises homelessness or housing exclusion into 4 categories which are adopted into this preliminary model. These are namely roofless, Houseless, Insecure housing, Inadequate housing.
